DRAM I-V Data
============================================================
Shape: (1718, 5)

Columns:
  0: spacer_length (nm)      - [15, 35]
  1: doping_conc (cm^-3)     - [1e16, 1e17, 1e18, 1e19]
  2: gate_length (nm)        - [5, 10, 20, 40, 80]
  3: voltage (V)             - [-2.0, 2.0]
  4: current (A)             - [-1.02e-16, 1.18e-05]

Usage:
  data = np.load('dram_data.npy')
  spacer = data[:, 0]
  doping = data[:, 1]
  gate = data[:, 2]
  voltage = data[:, 3]
  current = data[:, 4]
